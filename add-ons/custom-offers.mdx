Turn your website into an even more powerful platform by adding your own offers.

This Addon requires Pocket GPT Script v4.1 and above to be installed beforehand.

<img height="350" src="/images/add-on-custom-offers-inline.png" />

Designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing Pocket script Installation, this add-on brings an option to add your own offers to your platform.

## Features
- Supports YouTube Videos
- Custom Reward per Video
- Minimum Watch Duration with Timer 
- Video Pause and User Distraction Protection
- Additional Instructions Support 
- Add Unlimited Videos

Make your platform to support Watch & Earn today!

## Add-on Installation and Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Hosting/cPanel">
    1. Go to `yourdomain.com/cpanel`
    2. Enter your **username** and **password**
    Alternatively, you can use an FTP client as well.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload the Files">
    Locate the `oxy-web-videos.zip` file in your Pocket Script package
      - Upload the contents of oxy-web-videos.zip inside the addons folder
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in to Pocket Admin Interface">
    Open your web browser and navigate to your Pocket Script admin dashboard.
      - You'll be shown a notice/alert to install the Addon
      - Click the Activate Button on the notice to activate the addon
      - Once Activated, Navigate to Videos Section on the Admin sidebar to access this addon features
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's It!

Turn your website into an even more powerful platform by adding your own offers.

This Addon requires Pocket GPT Script v4.1 and above to be installed beforehand.

<img height="350" src="/images/add-on-custom-offers-inline.png" />

Designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing Pocket script Installation, this add-on brings an option to add your own offers to your platform.

## Features
- Geo targeting
- Custom Reward per Action
- Multi Events - Coming Soon
- Dynamic `{user}` and `{click_id}` Parameter Support 
- Supports Automatic Offer Expiry with expiry date
- Add Unlimited Offers

Add your own offers to your platform today!

## Installation and Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Hosting/cPanel">
    1. Go to `yourdomain.com/cpanel`
    2. Enter your **username** and **password**
    Alternatively, you can use an FTP client as well.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload the Files">
    Locate the `oxy-custom-offers.zip` file from the downloaded package
      - Upload the contents of `oxy-custom-offers.zip` inside the `admin -> addons` folder
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in to Pocket Admin Interface">
    Open your web browser and navigate to your Pocket Script admin dashboard.
      - You'll be shown an alert to activate the addon
      - Click the Activate Button on the alert to activate the addon
      - Once Activated, navigate to Custom Offers Section on the Admin sidebar to access this addon's features
  </Step>
</Steps>

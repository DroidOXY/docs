description: White label your platform by uploading your logos and texts
---

Follow these steps to upload Site Logos :

  1. Log in to your Admin Interface (Pocket Script Instance)
  2. Navigate to `Settings --> Configuration --> Web App Configuration` from Admin Sidebar
  3. Upload Site `Favicon` , `Dark Logo` and `Light Logo`

That's It!

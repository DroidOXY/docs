---
title: Emails/SMTP Configuration
description: Configure outgoing email settings, delivery services and otp verifications
---

Follow these steps to configure email settings :

  1. Log in to your Admin Interface (Pocket Script Instance)
  2. Navigate to `Settings --> Configuration --> Email SMTP Settings` from Admin Sidebar
  3. Choose Email Delivery Service
  4. Configure the rest

That's It!
